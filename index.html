About Me

Francisco Mena is a Master of Science (MSc) graduate from Federico Santa MarÃ­a Technical University (UTFSM, in Chile) where he also studied for his Bachelor of Science in the field of Computer Engineering specializing in Artificial Intelligence. Always interested in building automated systems that help society, he joined the Chilean Virtual Observatory (CHiVO) during his undergraduate studies in 2017. Later during the Master's program (from 2018) he explored three different areas using Artificial Intelligence: Crowdsourcing, Astroinformatics, and Information Retrieval (Similarity Search). He moved to Germany at the end of 2021 to pursue his doctorate in Computer science, specifically in Artificial Intelligence for Earth Observation (AI4EO). He is studying in Kaiserslautern, at the University of Kaiserslautern-Landau (RPTU) and working at the German Research Centre for Artificial Intelligence (DFKI) in the Smart Data & Knowledge Services (SDS) department under Prof. Dengel inside a Satellite Applications group (AI4EO Solution Factory).
