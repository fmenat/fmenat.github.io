About Me

MSc and BSc in Informatics Engineering

Francisco Mena is a Master of Science (MSc) graduate from Federico Santa MarÃ­a Technical University (UTFSM, in Chile) where he also studied for his Bachelor of Science in the field of Computer Engineering specializing in Artificial Intelligence. Always interested in building automated systems that help society, he joined the Chilean Virtual Observatory (CHiVO) during his undergraduate studies in 2017. Later during the Master's program (from 2018) he explored three different areas using Artificial Intelligence: Crowdsourcing, Astroinformatics, and Information Retrieval (Similarity Search). Once he obtained his Master's degree he started working as a Part-time Lecturer at the same institution where he studied (UTFSM) in 2020, teaching the course Computational Statistics and Artificial Neural Networks.

He now lives in Germany to pursue his doctorate in Computer science, specifically in Artificial Intelligence for Earth Observation (AI4EO). He is studying in Kaiserslautern, at the University of Kaiserslautern-Landau (RPTU) and working at the German Research Centre for Artificial Intelligence (DFKI) in the Smart Data & Knowledge Services (SDS) department under Prof. Dengel in a satellite applications group (AI4EO Solution Factory).
